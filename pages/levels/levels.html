How To Play

Select Your Difficulty

Choose your level of difficulty to begin your journey:

  • Easy: Prehistoric Era
  • Medium: Middle Ages
  • Hard: The Last Century

Match the Pairs

Click on two cards to reveal their contents. Your goal is to find pairs that contain the same ability, discovery or invention.

Complete the Level

Continue matching pairs until you've successfully paired all the cards on the grid. Each successful match brings us one step closer to safeguarding our collective wisdom.

Remember

Time is of the essence. The amnesia virus threatens to erase our past. Your sharp memory and quick thinking are our only hope. The world is counting on you to be the savior of knowledge and heritage.

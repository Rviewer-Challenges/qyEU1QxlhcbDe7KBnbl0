The Fallen Meteorite

Two weeks ago, a mysterious meteorite plummeted from the depths of space, its fiery descent was a spectacle for all to witness. At first, its arrival seemed like an extraordinary event, captivating the world's attention.
Little did we know that it brought with it a silent and insidious threat.

The Silent Threat Unleashed

In the days that followed, as the world marveled at the celestial spectacle, an invisible menace was quietly unleashed upon humanity. It started innocuously, with people experiencing slight memory lapses. But on the fourth day, it became alarmingly clear that something far more sinister was afoot.

The Ravages of Amnesia

As the days turned into weeks, an amnesia virus rapidly spread, erasing memories and knowledge
that had been painstakingly accumulated over millennia. Our history, our achievements, our very identity as a species hung in the balance, slipping through our fingers like grains of sand.

A Ray of Hope

Amid the growing despair, a glimmer of hope emerged. A group of dedicated individuals devised a daring plan to salvage humanity's knowledge and heritage. They created a game, a game that would challenge the very fabric of memory and intellect.
